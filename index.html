About this site

In medicine we use the results of clinical trials to make informed treatments about which treatments work best. But there is a problem: the results of clinical trials are being routinely and legally withheld from doctors, researchers, and patients. This is a problem for industry sponsored trials, and for trials funded by governments and charities.

We have ranked all the world’s major drug companies, and a selection of smaller firms, by the commitments they make to clinical trials transparency. Next, we will be ranking the policies of non-industry trial funders. You can find out more about unreported clinical trials at AllTrials.net.
